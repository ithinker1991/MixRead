Testing Library Features

You can add words to your personal library for later review. This feature helps you keep track of vocabulary you want to learn. Click the "Add to Library" button when you see highlighted words that interest you.

The batch marking feature allows you to quickly mark multiple words as known or unknown. This is especially useful when reviewing articles with many highlighted terms.
