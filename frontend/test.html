How to use this page:
  1. Make sure the MixRead extension is loaded and the backend server is running
  2. Reload this page or click the MixRead icon to activate highlighting
  3. You should see difficult words highlighted in yellow
  4. Click on highlighted words to see their definitions
  5. Use the popup to adjust difficulty level

Example 1: Mixed Difficulty Words

The extraordinary phenomenon was quite remarkable. Beautiful flowers bloomed throughout the garden. The comprehensive analysis demonstrated exceptional results. Simple solutions often prove most effective. Language learning requires consistent dedication and extraordinary effort.

Example 2: Common and Difficult Words Mixed

I walked through the town yesterday and saw something magnificent. The architecture was stunning, with intricate designs and elaborate decorations. Despite the weather being unfavorable, many people were there, enjoying themselves. The exhibition was absolutely phenomenal and surpassed all expectations.

Example 3: Reading Comprehension Practice

Climate change represents one of the most consequential challenges facing humanity. The ramifications extend across multiple domains including agriculture, infrastructure, and biodiversity. Scientists have documented unprecedented temperature fluctuations and volatile weather patterns. Mitigating these effects requires comprehensive international cooperation and substantial investment in sustainable technologies. The imperative to act is becoming increasingly urgent as we observe accelerating environmental degradation.

Example 4: Business Text

The company's strategic initiative emphasized innovation and operational efficiency. Management implemented robust procedures to enhance productivity while maintaining quality standards. Employees received comprehensive training programs to facilitate professional development. The organization established ambitious targets for revenue growth and market expansion. These endeavors culminated in remarkable performance metrics that exceeded projections.

Tips for Testing

  • Difficulty A1: Should highlight most words (basic level)
  • Difficulty B1: Should highlight intermediate words (about 30-50% of text)
  • Difficulty C1: Should highlight only advanced words (about 10-20% of text)
  • Click to add: Click highlighted words and press "Add to Library" to save them
  • View stats: Check the MixRead popup to see your vocabulary count
